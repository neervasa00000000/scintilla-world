Floty

Send questions from any page to a chat tab you choose (ChatGPT, etc.). Replies are copied here and shown in the bubble panel.

Floty does not switch browser tabs. It only reads what the chat page has already drawn. If a site pauses when its tab is fully covered (common on Gemini), keep that chat visible (split screen or second monitor) — you can stay on your other tab.

After changing this, refresh open tabs (or open new ones).
