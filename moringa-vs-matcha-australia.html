Key Differences

Caffeine

Moringa has zero caffeine - perfect for evening use, pregnancy, or those avoiding stimulants. Matcha contains 70mg per serve (like a coffee) with L-theanine for calmer energy.

Price

Moringa: $10-18 per 100g (Australian). Matcha: $30-80 per 100g (imported). Moringa is 3-4x better value per gram.
