Moringa vs Spirulina

🏆 Moringa wins overall for nutrition & value

The two most popular green superfoods in Australia, compared head-to-head. We break down nutrition, benefits, taste, and price.

Quick Verdict

Moringa wins for overall nutrition density, iron, calcium, vitamin A, skin health, and value. Better for daily wellness.

Spirulina wins for protein content (65%) and muscle recovery. Better for athletes needing high protein.

Nutrition Comparison (per 100g)

Nutrient Moringa Spirulina Winner
Protein 25g 57-65g ✓ Spirulina
Iron 28-36mg ✓ 28-30mg Moringa
Calcium 2,000mg ✓ 120mg Moringa (17x)
Vitamin A 18,000 IU ✓ 570 IU Moringa (32x)
Vitamin C 220mg ✓ 0mg Moringa
B12 0μg 130μg ✓ Spirulina
Antioxidants 50,000+ ✓ 25,000 Moringa (2x)

Which Should You Choose?

Choose Moringa if you want: Better overall nutrition, more iron/calcium, better skin health, zero caffeine, better taste, better value ($10-18 vs $18-30).

Choose Spirulina if you want: Maximum protein (65%), B12 supplement, muscle recovery, and don't mind the strong taste.
