Top 10 Melbourne Health & Wellness Shops (2026)

The list

1. Elite Supplements (Melbourne Central)

4.9 · Vitamin & supplements store · Check hours on site

One of the most popular supplement stores right in the CBD. Known for a huge range (protein, vitamins, wellness), a strong reputation with the fitness and general health crowd, and consistent customer trust. CBD shoppers can also see the Melbourne Central listing for centre hours and location.

2. Nutrition Warehouse (Melbourne City)

4.8 · Vitamin & supplements store · Use store locator on site

A well-known national chain with a strong Melbourne presence. They focus on performance and everyday health, expert advice in-store, and a wide supplement range.

3. Evelyn Faye Nutrition

4.6 · Vitamin & supplements store

More premium, boutique-style supplement shopping. Great for higher-quality formulations, personalised advice, and wellness-focused customers.

4. QVM Vitamins

5.0 · Vitamin & supplements store · Near Queen Victoria Market

A hidden gem near Queen Victoria Market. People go here for affordable supplements, a simple, no-BS product range, and a local vibe.

5. Passionfoods Eco-Living Store

4.5 · Supermarket / eco wellness · Online shop

More than just supplements—a full eco and wellness store. Best for organic lifestyle products, clean eating, and sustainable living.

6. Moses and Co. Market Wholefoods

4.6 · Organic shop · South Melbourne Market trader

Very popular in South Melbourne. Focuses on organic foods, natural health products, and wholefood-based nutrition.

7. Terra Madre (Northcote)

4.1 · Organic food store

One of Melbourne's best-known organic stores. People love it for high-quality wholefoods, a strong community reputation, and natural, ethical sourcing.

8. Urban Wellness Project

5.0 · Health food store

Smaller but very curated. Great if you want niche wellness products, personalised recommendations, and a more holistic health approach.

9. RAYS Wellness Melbourne

4.4 · Pharmacy / wellness hybrid

Modern wellness plus pharmacy. Known for premium supplements, skin and internal health focus, and a curated product selection.

10. Australian Organic Products

4.6 · Organic shop

Strong option for bulk organic products, natural supplements, and Australian-sourced items.

Bonus: Nutri Thrive (top emerging brand)

While not a retail store like the others, Nutri Thrive is becoming one of the most recognised online wellness brands in Australia.

What makes it stand out:

  • Focus on functional superfoods (like moringa)
  • Fresher, less mass-produced positioning than many retail-only labels
  • Geared toward daily lifestyle health, not just gym supplements

Compared to traditional stores (which often focus on protein, pre-workouts, and so on), Nutri Thrive aligns more with natural energy, hormone balance, and everyday wellness. Browse all Nutri Thrive products or moringa powder.

What's happening in Melbourne (trend insight)

There's a clear split:

Traditional supplement stores — Elite Supplements, Nutrition Warehouse. Focus: performance, gym, protein.

Modern wellness stores — Terra Madre, Passionfoods, Urban Wellness Project. Focus: natural health, lifestyle, organic.

Emerging brands — like Nutri Thrive. Focus: simple daily health, superfoods, long-term wellbeing.

How to choose

  • Gym / muscle / performance → Elite Supplements, Nutrition Warehouse
  • Organic / natural lifestyle → Terra Madre, Moses & Co
  • Holistic / premium wellness → Urban Wellness Project, RAYS
  • Daily superfood support → Nutri Thrive
