The “Locked Text” Struggle

Lecture slides, scanned notes, and contracts often arrive as PDFs that don’t allow simple copy-paste. OCR (optical character recognition) can free the text, but most tools make you jump through hoops.

Why Most Converters Suck

  • Tiny quotas: One file a day, then a paywall.
  • Destroyed formatting: Broken paragraphs, missing lines, weird characters.
