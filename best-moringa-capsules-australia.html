Capsule FAQ

Are the capsules gelatin or vegan?

Vegan. We use HPMC (hydroxypropyl methylcellulose) capsules — plant-derived, no animal products.

Can I open the capsules and use the powder?

Yes. The capsules easily pull apart. This is useful if you want to add moringa to a smoothie but only have capsules on hand.

How many capsules equal a teaspoon of powder?

Roughly 6 capsules = 1 teaspoon (3g) of powder. 2 capsules = 1/3 teaspoon. Use this to convert between forms.

Are capsules as effective as powder?

Yes — identical moringa powder inside. Absorption may be marginally slower (needs capsule to dissolve) but the nutrients are the same.

Can I take capsules on an empty stomach?

We recommend with food. Moringa is fiber-rich — taking with a meal aids digestion and reduces chance of mild stomach upset.
