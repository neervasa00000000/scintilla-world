Organize Your Thoughts Visually with CORE

CORE is a free holographic thought engine that turns scattered ideas into structured visual mind maps. Brainstorm, plan, and organize your thinking in an immersive 3D-style interface — all inside your browser, no account needed.

What Is a Thought Engine?

A thought engine is more than a mind map. It combines spatial visualization with real-time node creation, letting you place ideas in a 3D-like space where relationships become visible. Unlike flat list-based tools, CORE lets you see the shape of your thinking.

How to Use CORE

  1. Click anywhere on the canvas to create a new thought node
  2. Drag nodes to rearrange and group related ideas
  3. Use the mass, spin, and scale controls to adjust the visual weight of each node
  4. Export or screenshot your completed thought map

Why CORE Is Different

Most brainstorming tools force you into rigid hierarchies. CORE gives you spatial freedom — place ideas wherever they feel right, then discover the natural clusters that emerge. It is privacy-first: your thoughts never leave your browser.

Tips for Better Thought Mapping

Start with a single central idea and branch outward. Use short labels — two or three words per node — so the map stays readable at a glance. Group related nodes close together and connect them visually. Review your thought map after a break; fresh eyes reveal clusters you missed during the initial brainstorm. Export or screenshot your map to share with collaborators or revisit later.
