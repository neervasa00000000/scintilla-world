Design Tips for Better Digital Cards

Keep your message short — digital cards compete with notifications and feeds. Use high contrast between text and background for readability on small screens. Stick to two fonts maximum (one display, one body). Leave generous white space; a cramped card looks unprofessional. Finally, always test how your card looks at both mobile and desktop sizes before sending or sharing.
