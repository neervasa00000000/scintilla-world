How do I use moringa powder? +

Start with 1 teaspoon (3g) daily. Mix into smoothies, sprinkle on salads, stir into soups, or blend with juice. The earthy taste pairs well with citrus, banana, or mango. Avoid heating above 60°C to preserve nutrients. Gradually increase to 1 tablespoon after 2 weeks.

Is moringa safe for everyone? +

Moringa is generally safe for most adults. Avoid during pregnancy (can stimulate uterine contractions). Consult your doctor if taking blood thinners, diabetes medication, or have existing health conditions. Start with small doses to test tolerance.

Can I grow moringa in Australia? +

Yes, moringa grows in tropical and subtropical Australian climates (zones 9-11). It thrives in QLD, NT, and northern WA. In cooler regions like Melbourne or Sydney, grow in pots that can be moved indoors during winter. Bunnings occasionally stocks seedlings seasonally.
